Doritos Taco Salad

Description

A less healthy take on a normally healthy dinner. This taco salad, like most can be made with ground beef or chicken, whatever is preferred. The same applies to the doritos, I prefer the sweet and spicy chili flavor. It's a pretty simple and quick-to-make recipe that I throw together in my household in a few minutes when I don't feel like preparing foor for too long.

Ingredients

  • Beef or chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Doritos
  • Seasonings
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Avocados
  • Black olives
  • Whatever other toppings you desire

Steps

  1. Season meat and cut/shred if necessary
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Crush doritos
  5. Mix ingredients in large bowl
  6. Add salsa and sour cream as desired
  7. Serve in bowls and enjoy
