Monte Cristo

Description

The monte cristo is a classic I was taught by an old southern woman. It's the perfect mixture of sweet and salty. It's essentially a french toast sandwich with meat, cheese, and jelly. A very filling breakfast on its own, but it can be served with homefries or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Deli meat (I use turkey)
  • Cheese (I use swiss)
  • Eggs
  • Mustard
  • Jelly
  • Powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into bowl and whisk
  2. Assemble meat, cheese, and mustard into sandwich
  3. Dip bread in eggs and pan fry sandwich
  4. Cook until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted
  5. Cook any desired sides as well
  6. Serve with jelly on side and powdered sugar on bread
