Fish and Chips

Description

Fish and chips is a classic recipe from the United Kingdom. It includes strips of fried fish and french fries, which are called chips in the United Kingdom. Normally, Cod is the fish of choice, but any fried fish you like can be used.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Fish

Steps

  1. Slice and fry the potatoes to make the chips then let cool
  2. Make batter by mixing flour, baking powder, seasonings, milk, and eggs
  3. Coat the fish in the batter
  4. Fry the fish until golden brown
  5. Fry the potatoes a second time to make them crispy
  6. Serve alongside the fish
